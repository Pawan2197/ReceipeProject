Dish Type: Beverage
Dish Name: Orange Juice
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
* Oranges (freshly squeezed)                 - 2 cups
* Water                                      - 1/2 cup (optional)
* Sugar                                      - 1 tablespoon (optional)
* Ice cubes                                  - As needed

Cooking Time: 5 minutes

Procedure:
1. Juice: Squeeze fresh oranges to extract juice. Strain if necessary.
2. Serve: Mix with water (if preferred), add sugar, and serve over ice.
