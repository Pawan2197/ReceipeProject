
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Dal Tadka

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Yellow lentils (toor dal)               - 1 cup
    * Onion                                   - 1, chopped
    * Tomato                                  - 1, chopped
    * Spices (cumin, turmeric)                - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Ghee                                    - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 30 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Cook dal with turmeric and salt until soft.
    2. In another pan, heat ghee and add cumin, onion, and tomato.
    3. Pour tadka over cooked dal and stir well.
    4. Serve with rice or roti.
