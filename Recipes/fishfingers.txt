
Non-veg

Dish Type
Snacks

Dish name 
Fish Fingers

Servings
4-6

Ingredients

* Fish fillets (cod, tilapia)         - 1 pound 
* All-purpose flour                   - 1 cup
* Breadcrumbs                         - 1 cup
* Egg                                 - 1 beaten
* Salt                                - 1 teaspoon
* Oil                                 - For frying

Cooking Time 
10-12 minutes (frying)

Procedure

Prepare fish: Cut fish fillets into strips.
Coat fish fingers: Dredge fish strips in flour, then dip in beaten egg, and finally coat with breadcrumbs.
Fry: Fry the coated fish fingers in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
