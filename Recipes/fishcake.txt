Non-veg

Dish Type
Snacks

Dish name 
Fish Cake

Servings
4-6

Ingredients

* Flaked fish (salmon, tuna)       - 1 pound
* Potatoes                         - 1 large, boiled and mashed
* Onion                            - 1/2 cup, chopped
* Breadcrumbs                      - 1/2 cup
* Egg                              - 1 beaten
* Salt                             - 1 teaspoon
* Oil                              - For frying

Cooking Time 
10-12 minutes (frying)

Procedure

Combine ingredients: Combine flaked fish, mashed potatoes, onion, breadcrumbs, egg, and salt. Mix well.
Shape cakes: Shape the mixture into small cakes.
Fry: Fry the cakes in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
