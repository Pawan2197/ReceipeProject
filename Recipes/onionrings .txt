Non-veg

Dish Type: Snacks

Dish Name: Onion Rings

Servings: 10

Ingredients:

Onion                  - 1 large, sliced into rings
All-purpose flour      - 1 cup
Egg                    - 1 beaten
Breadcrumbs            - 1 cup
Salt                   - 1 teaspoon
Oil                    - For frying

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Procedure:

Prepare onion rings: Slice onion into rings of desired thickness.
Coat onion rings: Coat the onion rings in a mixture of egg, bread crumbs, and spices.
Fry: Fry the coated onion rings in hot oil until they are golden brown and Scrispy.
