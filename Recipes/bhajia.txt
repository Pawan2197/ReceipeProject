Veg

Dish type : Snacks

Dish name : Bhajia

Servings : 10

Ingredients :

* Onion                                 -  1 large, thinly sliced
* Gram flour                            -  1 cup
* Water                                 -  1/4 cup
* Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric)   -  1 teaspoon each
* Salt                                  -  1 teaspoon
* Oil                                   -  For frying

Cooking Time :
25 minutes

Procedure :

Make the batter: Follow the same procedure as for pakoras.
Coat onion: Dip sliced onion into the batter.
Fry: Fry the coated onion until golden brown and crispy.
