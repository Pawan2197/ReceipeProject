
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Veg Pulao

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Basmati rice                            - 1 cup
    * Mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)  - 1 cup
    * Spices (cumin, bay leaf)                - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 30 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Saute spices, add vegetables, and cook for 5 minutes.
    2. Add rice, water, and salt, then cook covered until rice is done.
    3. Serve hot with raita.
