Dish Type: Dessert
Dish Name: Vanilla Pudding
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
* Milk                                        - 2 cups
* Sugar                                       - 1/2 cup
* Cornstarch                                  - 3 tablespoons
* Vanilla extract                             - 1 teaspoon

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Procedure:
1. Heat: In a saucepan, whisk together milk, sugar, and cornstarch. Cook over medium heat until thickened.
2. Serve: Remove from heat, stir in vanilla extract, and pour into bowls. Chill before serving.
