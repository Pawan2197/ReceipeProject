
Veg

Dish Type: Snacks

Dish Name: Poha

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

* Poha (flattened rice)                    - 2 cups
* Onion (finely chopped)                   - 1 medium
* Green chili (finely chopped)             - 1-2
* Curry leaves                             - 6-8
* Mustard seeds                            - 1 teaspoon
* Turmeric powder                          - 1/2 teaspoon
* Peanuts                                  - 1/4 cup
* Oil                                      - 2 tablespoons
* Lemon juice                              - 1 tablespoon
* Sugar                                    - 1/2 teaspoon (optional)
* Salt                                     - to taste
* Fresh coriander (chopped)                - for garnish

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Procedure:

1. Prepare Poha: Rinse poha in water gently until it softens. Drain completely and set aside.

2. Saute Ingredients: Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them crackle. Toss in curry leaves, green chilies, and peanuts. Saute until peanuts turn golden brown.

3. Cook Aromatics: Add onions and Saute until they turn translucent. Stir in turmeric powder.

4. Combine Poha: Add softened poha to the pan, followed by salt and sugar (if using). Mix gently to avoid breaking the poha. Cook for 2-3 minutes on low heat.

5. Garnish and Serve: Turn off the heat, add lemon juice, and mix well. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot.
