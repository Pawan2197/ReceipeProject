Dish Type: Dessert
Dish Name: Cheesecake
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
* Cream cheese                                - 1 cup
* Sugar                                       - 1/2 cup
* Whipped cream                               - 1 cup
* Graham cracker crust                        - 1 (store-bought)

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Procedure:
1. Mix: Blend cream cheese, sugar, and whipped cream until smooth.
2. Assemble: Spread mixture over crust and refrigerate for 4 hours before serving.
