	
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Moong Dal Tadka

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Moong dal (split green gram)            - 1 cup
    * Onion                                   - 1, chopped
    * Tomato                                  - 1, chopped
    * Spices (cumin, turmeric)                - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Ghee                                    - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 20 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Boil dal with turmeric and salt.
    2. Prepare tadka with cumin, onion, and tomato, pour over dal.
    3. Serve with rice or chapati.
