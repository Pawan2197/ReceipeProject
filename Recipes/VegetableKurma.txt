
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Vegetable Kurma

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Mixed vegetables                        - 1 cup
    * Coconut milk                            - 1/2 cup
    * Onion                                   - 1, chopped
    * Tomato                                  - 1, chopped
    * Spices (garam masala, turmeric)         - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 30 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Saute onion, add tomato and spices, cook until soft.
    2. Add vegetables and coconut milk, simmer for 10 minutes.
    3. Serve with rice or chapati.
