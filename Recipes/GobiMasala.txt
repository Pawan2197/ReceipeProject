
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Gobi Masala

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Cauliflower                             - 1 medium, cut into florets
    * Onion                                   - 1, chopped
    * Tomato                                  - 1, chopped
    * Spices (turmeric, cumin, garam masala)  - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 25 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Saute onion and spices until golden.
    2. Add cauliflower and cook until tender.
    3. Add tomatoes, cook until flavors meld.
    4. Serve with chapati or rice.
