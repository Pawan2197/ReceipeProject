Dish Type: Beverage
Dish Name: Cold Coffee
Servings: 1

Ingredients:
* Coffee (brewed, chilled)                   - 1 cup
* Milk                                       - 1/2 cup
* Sugar                                      - 1 teaspoon (optional)
* Ice cubes                                  - 1/2 cup

Cooking Time: 5 minutes

Procedure:
1. Combine: In a glass, mix chilled coffee with milk and sugar.
2. Serve: Add ice cubes and enjoy.
