Non-Veg

Dish Type
Snacks

Dish name 
Chicken Tikka

Servings
4-6

Ingredients

* Chicken breast         - 1 pound
* Yogurt                 - 1/2 cup
* Lemon juice            - 1 tablespoon
* Ginger-garlic paste    - 1 teaspoon
* Tandoori masala        - 1 teaspoon
* Turmeric               - 1/4 teaspoon
* Salt                   - 1 teaspoon
* Oil                    - For grilling

Cooking Time 
15-20 minutes (grilling)

Procedure

Marinate chicken: Cut chicken breast into bite-sized pieces.
In a bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, tandoori masala, turmeric, and salt. 
Marinate chicken pieces in this mixture for at least 30 minutes, or preferably overnight.

Grill chicken: Preheat a grill or grill pan. Thread marinated chicken pieces onto skewers. 
Grill the skewers, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
