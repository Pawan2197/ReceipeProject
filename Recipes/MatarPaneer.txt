
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Matar Paneer

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Paneer                                  - 200 grams, cubed
    * Green peas                              - 1 cup
    * Onion                                   - 1, chopped
    * Tomato                                  - 2, pureed
    * Spices (garam masala, chili powder)     - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 30 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Saute onions until golden brown, add tomato puree and spices.
    2. Add green peas, cook until peas are tender.
    3. Add paneer and salt, simmer for 10 minutes.
    4. Serve hot with naan or rice.
