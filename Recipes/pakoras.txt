Veg

Dish Type: Snacks
Dish Name: Pakoras
Servings: 10

Ingredients:

* Gram flour                                           - 1 cup
* Water                                                - 1/4 cup
* Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric)                  - 1 teaspoon each
* Salt                                                 - 1 teaspoon
* Vegetables (onion, potato, cauliflower, spinach)     - 1 cup, chopped
* Oil

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Procedure:

1. Make the batter: Combine gram flour, water, spices, and salt in a bowl. Mix until smooth.
2. Coat vegetables: Dip chopped vegetables into the batter, ensuring they are evenly coated.
3. Fry: Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot. Fry the coated vegetables until golden brown and crispy.
