Dish Type: Dessert
Dish Name: Apple Pie
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
* Apples (sliced)                             - 3 cups
* Sugar                                       - 1/2 cup
* Cinnamon                                    - 1 teaspoon
* Pie crust                                   - 1 (store-bought or homemade)
* Butter                                      - 1 tablespoon

Cooking Time: 40 minutes

Procedure:
1. Prepare: Place apple slices in a pie crust, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon, and dot with butter.
2. Bake: Cover with a top crust, seal edges, and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-35 minutes until golden brown.
