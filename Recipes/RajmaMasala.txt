
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Rajma Masala

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Red kidney beans (rajma)                - 1 cup, soaked overnight
    * Onion                                   - 1 large, chopped
    * Tomato                                  - 2, pureed
    * Spices (rajma masala, chili powder)     - 1 tablespoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 40 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Pressure cook kidney beans until soft.
    2. Saute onions, add tomato puree and spices; cook until thick.
    3. Add kidney beans, simmer for 15 minutes.
    4. Serve with rice or roti.
