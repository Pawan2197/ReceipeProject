
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Mixed Vegetable Curry

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas)  - 2 cups
    * Onion                                   - 1, chopped
    * Tomato                                  - 1, chopped
    * Spices (turmeric, garam masala)         - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 30 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Saute onions and spices until golden brown.
    2. Add vegetables, cook until tender.
    3. Add tomatoes, cook until soft and flavors combine.
    4. Serve hot with rice or roti.
