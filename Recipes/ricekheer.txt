Dish Type: Dessert
Dish Name: Rice Kheer
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
* Rice                                        - 1/4 cup
* Milk                                        - 4 cups
* Sugar                                       - 1/2 cup
* Cardamom powder                             - 1/4 teaspoon
* Nuts and raisins                            - For garnish

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Procedure:
1. Cook: Simmer rice and milk in a pot until thickened. Add sugar and cardamom powder.
2. Serve: Garnish with nuts and raisins and serve chilled or warm.
