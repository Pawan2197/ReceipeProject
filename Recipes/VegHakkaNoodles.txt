
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Veg Hakka Noodles

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Noodles                                 - 200 grams, boiled
    * Mixed vegetables (carrot, bell pepper)  - 1 cup, julienned
    * Soy sauce                               - 1 tablespoon
    * Green onion                             - 1, chopped
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 20 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Saute vegetables in oil on high heat.
    2. Add noodles, soy sauce, and salt; stir-fry for 2 minutes.
    3. Garnish with green onion and serve hot.
