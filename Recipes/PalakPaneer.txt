
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Palak Paneer

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Spinach                                 - 2 cups, blanched and pureed
    * Paneer                                  - 200 grams, cubed
    * Onion                                   - 1, chopped
    * Garlic                                  - 1 teaspoon, minced
    * Spices (garam masala, cumin)            - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 30 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Saute onion and garlic until soft.
    2. Add spinach puree and spices; cook for 5 minutes.
    3. Add paneer and salt, simmer for 10 minutes.
    4. Serve with rice or naan.
