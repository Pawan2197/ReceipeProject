Veg

Dish type : Snacks

Dish name : Aloo Tikki


Servings : 10


Ingredients : 

* Potatoes                                  -  2 large, boiled and mashed 
* Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric)       -  1 teaspoon each
* Salt                                      -  1 teaspoon
* Breadcrumbs                               -  1/4 cup
* Oil                                       -  For frying

Cooking Time :  30 minutes


Procedure : 
	

1. Mix ingredients: Combine mashed potatoes, spices, salt, and breadcrumbs in a bowl. Mix well.
2. Shape patties: Shape the mixture into small patties.
3. Fry: Fry the patties in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
