
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Bhindi Masala

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Okra (bhindi)                           - 250 grams, chopped
    * Onion                                   - 1, sliced
    * Spices (turmeric, cumin)                - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 25 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Saute spices and onion until golden.
    2. Add okra, cook until tender.
    3. Season with salt and serve with chapati.
