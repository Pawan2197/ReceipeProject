Dish Type: Dessert
Dish Name: Gulab Jamun
Servings: 12 pieces

Ingredients:
* Milk powder                                 - 1 cup
* All-purpose flour                           - 1/4 cup
* Baking soda                                 - 1/4 teaspoon
* Ghee                                        - 1 tablespoon
* Milk                                        - As needed
* Sugar                                       - 1 cup
* Water                                       - 1 cup
* Cardamom                                    - 2 pods

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Procedure:
1. Dough: Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee. Add milk to form a dough.
2. Syrup: Boil sugar, water, and cardamom to make syrup.
3. Fry: Shape dough into balls and fry until golden. Soak in syrup and serve.
