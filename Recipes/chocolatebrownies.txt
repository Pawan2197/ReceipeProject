Dish Type: Dessert
Dish Name: Chocolate Brownies
Servings: 8

Ingredients:
* Butter                                      - 1/2 cup
* Sugar                                       - 1 cup
* Cocoa powder                                - 1/3 cup
* All-purpose flour                           - 1/2 cup
* Eggs                                        - 2
* Vanilla extract                             - 1 teaspoon
* Salt                                        - 1/4 teaspoon
* Chocolate chips                             - 1/4 cup (optional)

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Procedure:
1. Mix: Melt butter and mix with sugar, cocoa powder, and salt. Add eggs and vanilla, stirring until smooth. Fold in flour and chocolate chips.
2. Bake: Pour into a greased baking pan and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20-25 minutes. Cool and slice into squares.
