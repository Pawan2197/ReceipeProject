
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Dum Aloo

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Baby potatoes                           - 10, boiled and peeled
    * Onion                                   - 1, chopped
    * Tomato                                  - 2, pureed
    * Spices (turmeric, garam masala)         - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 30 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Fry boiled potatoes until golden.
    2. Saute onion, add tomato puree and spices, cook until thick.
    3. Add potatoes and cook for 10 minutes.
    4. Serve with rice or naan.
