Dish Type: Dessert
Dish Name: Gajar Halwa
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
* Carrots (grated)                            - 2 cups
* Milk                                        - 1 cup
* Sugar                                       - 1/2 cup
* Ghee                                        - 2 tablespoons
* Cardamom powder                             - 1/4 teaspoon
* Nuts (optional)                             - For garnish

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Procedure:
1. Cook: In a pan, cook carrots with milk until softened. Add sugar and ghee, stirring well.
2. Garnish: Sprinkle cardamom powder and nuts before serving.
