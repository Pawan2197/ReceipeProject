
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Kadhi Pakora

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Gram flour                              - 1 cup
    * Yogurt                                  - 1 cup
    * Onion                                   - 1, sliced
    * Spices (turmeric, cumin)                - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - For frying

    Cooking Time: 40 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Make batter with gram flour and spices for pakoras and deep fry.
    2. Mix yogurt and gram flour for kadhi, simmer until thick.
    3. Add pakoras and cook for 10 minutes.
    4. Serve with rice.
