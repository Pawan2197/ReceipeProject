

Non -veg

Dish Type
Snacks

Dish name 
Chicken Nuggets

Servings
4-6

Ingredients

* Chicken breast         - 1 pound
* Breadcrumbs            - 1 cup
* All-purpose flour      - 1/2 cup
* Egg                    - 1 beaten
* Salt                   - 1 teaspoon
* Oil                    - For frying

Cooking Time 
10-12 minutes (frying)

Procedure

Prepare chicken: Cut chicken breast into bite-sized cubes.
Coat chicken nuggets: Dredge chicken cubes in flour, then dip in beaten egg, and finally coat with breadcrumbs.
Fry: Fry the coated chicken nuggets in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
