
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Cabbage Sabzi

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Cabbage                                 - 2 cups, shredded
    * Onion                                   - 1, chopped
    * Spices (turmeric, cumin)                - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 1 tablespoon

    Cooking Time: 15 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Saute cumin and onion until golden.
    2. Add cabbage and spices, cook until soft.
    3. Serve with roti or rice.
