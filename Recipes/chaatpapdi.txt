Dish Type: Snacks

Dish Name: Chaat Papdi

Servings: 10

Ingredients:

Papdi shells              - 10-15
Mashed potatoes           - 1 cup
Yogurt                    - 1/2 cup
Tamarind chutney          - 1/4 cup
Mint chutney              - 1/4 cup
Chaat masala              - 1 teaspoon

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Procedure:

Layer ingredients: In a bowl, layer papdi shells, mashed potatoes, yogurt, tamarind chutney, mint chutney, and chaat masala.
You can customize the layers and the amount of each ingredient to your liking.
Serve immediately: Serve the layered chaat immediately for the best taste and texture.
