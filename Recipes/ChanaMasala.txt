    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Chana Masala

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Chickpeas                               - 1 cup, soaked overnight
    * Onion                                   - 1, chopped
    * Tomato                                  - 2, pureed
    * Chana masala                            - 1 tablespoon
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 35 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Boil chickpeas until soft.
    2. Saute onion, add tomato puree and chana masala.
    3. Add chickpeas and simmer for 15 minutes.
    4. Serve with rice or bhature.
