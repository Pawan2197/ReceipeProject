Dish Type: Beverage
Dish Name: Lemonade
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
* Lemons (freshly squeezed)                  - 1/2 cup
* Water                                      - 4 cups
* Sugar                                      - 1/2 cup
* Ice cubes                                  - As needed
* Mint leaves                                - Optional (for garnish)

Cooking Time: 5 minutes

Procedure:
1. Mix: Combine lemon juice, water, and sugar in a pitcher. Stir well until sugar dissolves.
2. Serve: Pour over ice cubes in glasses and garnish with mint leaves.
