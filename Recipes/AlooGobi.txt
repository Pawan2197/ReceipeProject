Veg

Dish type: Main Course

Dish name: Aloo Gobi

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

* Potato                                  - 2 medium, diced
* Cauliflower                             - 1 cup, florets
* Onion                                   - 1, chopped
* Spices (turmeric, cumin)                - 1 teaspoon each
* Salt                                    - To taste
* Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Procedure:

1. Saute onion and spices until golden.
2. Add potatoes and cauliflower, cover and cook until soft.
3. Season with salt, stir occasionally until vegetables are tender.
4. Serve hot with chapati or rice.
