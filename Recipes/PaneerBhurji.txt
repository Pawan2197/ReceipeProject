
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Paneer Bhurji

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Paneer                                  - 200 grams, crumbled
    * Onion                                   - 1, chopped
    * Tomato                                  - 1, chopped
    * Spices (turmeric, chili powder)         - 1/2 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 1 tablespoon

    Cooking Time: 15 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Saute onion, add tomatoes and spices.
    2. Add crumbled paneer, cook for 5 minutes.
    3. Serve hot with roti.
