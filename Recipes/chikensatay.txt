Non-veg

Dish Type
Snacks

Dish name 
Chicken Satay

Servings
4-6

Ingredients
* Chicken breast      -1 pound
* Satay sauce         -1/2 cup
* Skewers             -10

Cooking Time 
10-12 minutes (grilling)

Procedure

Cut chicken: Cut chicken breast into thin strips.
Marinate chicken: Marinate chicken strips in satay sauce.
Thread chicken: Thread marinated chicken onto skewers.
Grill chicken: Grill the skewers until cooked through and slightly charred.
