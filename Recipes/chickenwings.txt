Dish Type
Snacks

Dish name 
Chicken Wings

Servings
4-6

Ingredients

* Chicken wings     - 1 pound
* Barbecue sauce    - 1/2 cup Hot sauce
* Garlic powder     - 1 tablespoon
* Oil               - 1 teaspoon(For baking or grilling)

Cooking Time 
25-30 minutes (baking or grilling)

Procedure

Preheat oven or grill: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C) or preheat a grill.
Season chicken wings: Toss chicken wings with barbecue sauce, hot sauce, and garlic powder.
Cook chicken wings: Bake or grill chicken wings until cooked through and slightly charred.
