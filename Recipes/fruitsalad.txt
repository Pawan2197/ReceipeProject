Dish Type: Dessert
Dish Name: Fruit Salad
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
* Mixed fruits (chopped)                      - 2 cups
* Honey                                       - 2 tablespoons
* Lemon juice                                 - 1 tablespoon
* Mint leaves                                 - Optional (for garnish)

Cooking Time: 5 minutes

Procedure:
1. Toss: Combine chopped fruits, honey, and lemon juice in a bowl.
2. Serve: Mix gently, garnish with mint leaves, and serve fresh.
