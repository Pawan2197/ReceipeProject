Dish Type: Beverage
Dish Name: Masala Chai
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
* Water                                      - 1 cup
* Milk                                       - 1 cup
* Tea leaves                                 - 2 teaspoons
* Sugar                                      - 2 teaspoons
* Spices (ginger, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon) - As needed

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Procedure:
1. Boil: Heat water with spices. Add tea leaves and boil for 2 minutes.
2. Add milk: Pour in milk and sugar. Simmer for 2-3 minutes.
3. Serve: Strain into cups and serve hot.
