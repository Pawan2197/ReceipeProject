Veg

Dish Type: Snacks
Dish Name: Samosa
Servings: 10

Ingredients:

Flour                                  - 2 cups
Water                                  - 1/2 cup
Salt                                   - 1 teaspoon
Potatoes                               - 2 large, boiled and mashed
Peas                                   - 1 cup
Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric)    - 1 teaspoon each
Oil                                    - For frying

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Procedure:

Make the dough: Combine flour, water, and salt in a bowl. Knead until smooth and elastic. Rest for 30 minutes.
Prepare the filling: Mix mashed potatoes, peas, and spices in a separate bowl.

Roll out the dough: Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin circle.
Cut and fill: Cut each circle into triangles. Place a spoonful of filling at the base of each triangle.

Fold and seal: Fold the sides of the triangle over the filling and seal the edges tightly.
Fry: Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot. Fry the samosas until golden brown and crispy.
