Dish Type: Dessert
Dish Name: Mango Mousse
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
* Mango puree                                 - 1 cup
* Whipped cream                               - 1 cup
* Gelatin                                     - 1 teaspoon
* Sugar                                       - 2 tablespoons

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Procedure:
1. Mix: Dissolve gelatin in warm water. Blend with mango puree and sugar.
2. Fold: Gently fold in whipped cream and refrigerate until set.
