
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Veg Fried Rice

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Cooked rice                             - 2 cups
    * Mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)  - 1 cup
    * Soy sauce                               - 1 tablespoon
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 15 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Saute vegetables in oil on high heat.
    2. Add rice, soy sauce, and salt; stir-fry until well mixed.
    3. Serve hot.
