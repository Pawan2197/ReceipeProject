
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Pumpkin Curry

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Pumpkin                                 - 2 cups, diced
    * Onion                                   - 1, chopped
    * Tomato                                  - 1, chopped
    * Spices (cumin, turmeric, garam masala)  - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 20 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Saute onion and spices until golden.
    2. Add pumpkin, cook until tender.
    3. Add tomatoes, cook until flavors meld.
    4. Serve with rice or chapati.
