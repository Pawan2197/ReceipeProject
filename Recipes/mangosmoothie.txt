Dish Type: Beverage
Dish Name: Mango Smoothie
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
* Mango pulp (fresh or canned)               - 1 cup
* Yogurt                                     - 1/2 cup
* Milk                                       - 1/2 cup
* Honey                                      - 1 tablespoon (optional)
* Ice cubes                                  - 1/2 cup

Cooking Time: 5 minutes

Procedure:
1. Blend: Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
2. Serve: Pour into glasses and enjoy chilled.
