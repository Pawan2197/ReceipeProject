    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Masoor Dal

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Red lentils (masoor dal)                - 1 cup
    * Onion                                   - 1, chopped
    * Tomato                                  - 1, chopped
    * Spices (cumin, turmeric)                - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 25 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Boil dal with salt and turmeric until soft.
    2. Prepare tadka with cumin, onion, and tomato, then pour over dal.
    3. Serve with rice or chapati.
