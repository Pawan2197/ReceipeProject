Dish Type: Snacks

Dish Name: Cheese Balls

Servings: 10

Ingredients:

Cheese (mozzarella, cheddar)         - 1 cup, shredded
Breadcrumbs                          - 1 cup
Egg                                  - 1, beaten
Flour                                - 1/4 cup
Salt                                 - 1 teaspoon
Oil                                  - For frying

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Procedure:

Shape cheese balls: Shape shredded cheese into small balls.
Coat cheese balls: Coat cheese balls in flour, then egg, and finally breadcrumbs.
Fry: Fry the coated cheese balls in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
