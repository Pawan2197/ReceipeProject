Non-veg

Dish Type
Snacks

Dish name 
Prawn Tempura

Servings
4-6

Ingredients
* Prawns      -  1 pound Tempura batter (pre-made or homemade)
* 1 cup Oil   -  For frying

Cooking Time 
2-3 minutes (frying)


Procedure

Prepare prawns: Devein and clean prawns.
Coat prawns: Dip prawns into tempura batter and fry in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
