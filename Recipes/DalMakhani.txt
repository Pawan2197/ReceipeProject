
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Dal Makhani

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Black lentils (urad dal)                - 1 cup
    * Kidney beans                            - 1/2 cup
    * Cream                                   - 1/2 cup
    * Spices (garam masala, chili powder)     - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Butter                                  - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 40 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Boil dal and kidney beans until soft.
    2. Add spices, cream, and butter, cook for 20 minutes.
    3. Serve hot with naan.
