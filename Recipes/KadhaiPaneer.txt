
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Kadai Paneer

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Paneer                                  - 200 grams, cubed
    * Bell peppers                            - 1 cup, diced
    * Onion                                   - 1 large, chopped
    * Tomato                                  - 2, pureed
    * Kadai masala                            - 2 teaspoons
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

    Cooking Time: 25 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Saute onion, bell peppers, and kadai masala.
    2. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
    3. Add paneer and salt, cook for 5 minutes.
    4. Serve with naan or roti.
