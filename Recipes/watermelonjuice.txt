Dish Type: Beverage
Dish Name: Watermelon juice
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
* Watermelon (seedless, diced)               - 2 cups
* Lemon juice                                - 1 tablespoon
* Sugar                                      - 1 tablespoon (optional)
* Mint leaves                                - Optional (for garnish)
* Ice cubes                                  - As needed

Cooking Time: 5 minutes

Procedure:
1. Blend: Blend watermelon, lemon juice, and sugar until smooth.
2. Serve: Pour over ice cubes and garnish with mint leaves if desired.
