
    Veg

    Dish type: Main Course

    Dish name: Malai Kofta

    Servings: 4

    Ingredients:

    * Paneer                                  - 100 grams, grated
    * Potato                                  - 1 large, boiled and mashed
    * Cream                                   - 1/2 cup
    * Tomato                                  - 2, pureed
    * Spices (garam masala, chili powder)     - 1 teaspoon each
    * Salt                                    - To taste
    * Oil                                     - For frying

    Cooking Time: 35 minutes

    Procedure:

    1. Form koftas with paneer and potato, then deep fry.
    2. Prepare curry with tomato puree, cream, and spices.
    3. Add koftas and simmer for 5 minutes.
    4. Serve with naan or rice.
