Dish Type: Beverage
Dish Name: Hot Chocolate
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
* Milk                                       - 2 cups
* Cocoa powder                               - 2 tablespoons
* Sugar                                      - 2 tablespoons
* Dark chocolate (grated)                    - 2 tablespoons
* Whipped cream                              - Optional (for topping)

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Procedure:
1. Heat: Warm milk in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Mix: Whisk in cocoa powder, sugar, and grated chocolate until fully dissolved.
3. Serve: Pour into mugs and top with whipped cream if desired.
