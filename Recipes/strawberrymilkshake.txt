Dish Type: Beverage
Dish Name: Strawberry Milkshake
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
* Strawberries (fresh or frozen)             - 1 cup
* Milk                                       - 1 cup
* Sugar                                      - 2 tablespoons
* Ice cream (vanilla)                        - 1 scoop

Cooking Time: 5 minutes

Procedure:
1. Blend: Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
2. Serve: Pour into glasses and enjoy.
