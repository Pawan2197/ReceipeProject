Veg

Dish type: Main Course

Dish name: Baingan Bharta

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

* Eggplant                                - 1 large
* Onion                                   - 1, chopped
* Tomato                                  - 2, chopped
* Spices (chili powder, garam masala)     - 1 teaspoon each
* Salt                                    - To taste
* Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Procedure:

1. Roast eggplant until soft, peel, and mash.
2. Saute onions and spices, add tomatoes and cook until soft.
3. Add mashed eggplant, cook for 10 minutes.
4. Serve hot with roti.
