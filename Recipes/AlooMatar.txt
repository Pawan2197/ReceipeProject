Veg

Dish type: Main Course

Dish name: Aloo Matar

Servings: 4			

Ingredients:

* Potatoes                                - 2 large, diced
* Green peas                              - 1 cup
* Onion                                   - 1, chopped
* Tomato                                  - 1, chopped
* Spices (cumin, turmeric, garam masala)  - 1 teaspoon each
* Salt                                    - To taste
* Oil                                     - 2 tablespoons

Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Procedure:

1. Saute cumin seeds and onion until golden.
2. Add potatoes, green peas, and spices; cook for 15 minutes.
3. Add tomatoes and cook until potatoes are soft.
4. Serve with chapati or rice.
