Dish Type: Beverage
Dish Name: Mojito (Non-Alcoholic)
Servings: 1

Ingredients:
* Lime juice                                 - 2 tablespoons
* Sugar                                      - 1 teaspoon
* Mint leaves                                - 8-10
* Soda water                                 - 1 cup
* Ice cubes                                  - As needed

Cooking Time: 5 minutes

Procedure:
1. Muddle: In a glass, muddle mint leaves, lime juice, and sugar.
2. Serve: Add ice cubes, pour soda water, and mix gently.
